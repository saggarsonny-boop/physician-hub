Federal Healthcare Fraud Defense: Clinical Expert and Legal Scholar

Retained consulting for plaintiff and defense counsel in federal healthcare enforcement matters.

Federal healthcare fraud cases turn on clinical judgment, billing intent, and the gap between what administrators knew and what prosecutors allege. Most expert witnesses can speak to one of those. Dr Sonny Saggar MD speaks to all three, from the inside.

Oxford and Barts trained. 25 years of emergency medicine including hospital administration, ATLS instruction, and urgent care operations. Author of six published papers specifically on federal healthcare fraud enforcement, the post-Loper Bright regulatory landscape, intent standards under 18 USC 1347, and private credentialing fairness. SEAK registered expert witness. Active in both plaintiff and defense matters.

What makes this different: Dr Saggar has navigated the federal enforcement system as a defendant, as a physician, and as a scholar.
