Off-Label Promotion Defense and Pharmaceutical Fraud Consulting

Independent reads on clinical evidence integrity, regulatory exposure, and FDA compliance for pharmaceutical legal affairs and compliance departments.

Modern pharmaceutical enforcement actions frequently target off-label marketing, medical science liaison (MSL) activities, and scientific study integrity. Defending these claims requires a precise clinical evaluation of actual standard of care practices, coupled with a deep understanding of billing intent structures under federal enforcement frameworks.

Dr Sonny Saggar MD provides specialized clinical and regulatory consulting for legal counsel and compliance officers navigating pharmaceutical litigation and internal investigations.

The credential: Oxford and Barts. 25 years emergency medicine. Published scholar with twelve papers on healthcare enforcement, administrative law post-Loper Bright, digital health standards, and open medical coding systems. Direct experience navigating federal healthcare enforcement as both defendant and scholar.
